Paytm for Business offers 360-degree solutions that help you at every step of your entrepreneurial journey. Our solutions will help you accept payments, disburse payments, manage your business(s), and grow efficiently.

Payment Solutions for Accepting In-Store Payments

1. All-In-One QR Code: Accept a mobile payment through QR code in your retail shop instantly with our All-In-One QR. When you’re trying to spend minimally or are searching for something more versatile, bulky card readers are a costly way to take payments. With QR payment solutions, getting set up is much easier. This greatly improves the overall customer experience: while you’re at complete ease with one QR code for all payment types, customers just need to scan a code, insert their card details and complete the payment.

2. All-In-One POS: Accept payments from multiple sources using All-In-One POS. Creating a proper customer experience leads to better customer satisfaction, and a fast point of sale allows you to complete transactions faster. Paytm offers a robust POS machine inclusive of POS card payment options added to the POS card swipe machine. This ensures that consumers don’t have long wait times and thus forms an excellent advantage for them too.

3. Soundbox: Get notified of every payment received in-store via instant voice notification with Soundbox. This solution enables you to never miss another payment and offers multilingual support while supporting multiple payment sources. You can check more details about Paytm Soundbox here.

Collect Online Payments on Website, App or via Links

Paytm Payment Gateway enables you to accept online payments via any device through a single robust processing platform. Our payment gateway lets you collect payments on any website, mobile app, tablet, or even using any social app. While there are many channels through which you can accept payments, all your transactions are reconciled in one platform — the Paytm Payment Gateway.

In addition to our payment gateway, you can also accept payment via Payment Link and share it with your customers via WhatsApp, Facebook, Email, or SMS to collect online payments. For us at Paytm, no distance is too big to receive payments. The Paytm Payment Link enables you to collect online payments remotely from your customers, without needing to have a website or an app.

What Makes Paytm, the Best Payment Gateway service provider in India?

1. Go live instantly: You don't have to wait for hours to get started with Instant Activation. Our onboarding process is designed to fasten the account activation process, enabling you to accept online payments within minutes.

2. Get payments settled in your preferred bank account: With the Paytm Payment Gateway, you do not need to open a new bank account for collecting online payments. Simply provide the details of your preferred bank account at the time of onboarding, and we will ensure all your payments are settled in the same.

3. Widest Range of Payment Sources:

• Paytm App: Paytm Payment Gateway gives your customers freedom to pay with their favorite e-wallet app - the Paytm App.

• Debit/Credit Cards: Paytm Payment Gateway supports payments via all debit and credit cards. For added convenience, we also let customers save their card details for a seamless experience every time they visit your website/App.

• All UPI Apps: Paytm Payment Gateway supports UPI (Unified Payment Interface) payments. The UPI payment source is one of the most preferred payment sources among customers. This payment source is given as a default checkout option so that your customers don't have to share sensitive bank information to complete a transaction. With all the buzz around UPI payment gateway charges, the Paytm payment gateway takes pride in charging a 0% fee on all UPI payments.

• Netbanking: With the Paytm Payment Gateway, you can empower your customers to pay through net banking options from all leading banks.

• Paytm Postpaid: Paytm Payment Gateway gives you the edge over others by offering your customers the option to buy something now and pay for it later with Paytm Postpaid.

• EMI: Paytm Payment Gateway also supports EMI Payments to reduce upfront payments and make products affordable through flexible payment options for your customers.

4. Manage your business in one place with the powerful Business Dashboard:

Paytm Payment Gateway's robust Business Dashboard gives you full control over inbound & outbound cash flows, bank accounts, and events all in one place.

• Transactions Management: Manage all your business transactions, initiate refunds, solve disputes, and get settlements from one dashboard. You can also download reports and get useful insights in real-time to make informed business decisions.

• Bank Account Management: Our Business Dashboard lets you manage your account with ease. You can edit your business details, bank details, notification settings, and much more in a single place.

• Notifications: With the Paytm Payment Gateway, you will get notified of every event, ensuring you stay informed and can take timely actions on the go.

5. World-Class Security: Paytm Payment Gateway's anti-fraud technology is PCI-DSS compliant with 128-bit encryption and ensures that all your transactions are secure.

6. Your own international (payment) gateway to go global: Paytm Payment Gateway lets you accept international payments from 200+ countries at the best possible rates via major International Cards.
